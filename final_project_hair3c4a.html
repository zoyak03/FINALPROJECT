Curly Hair

Curly hair has been unappreciated and mistreated for too long so here are some tips and tricks to take care of your beautiful curls. Plus hairstyles and lots of curl love. There are many different tyes of curly hair on this page we'll cover all types equally.

3a-3b

This hair is made up of well-defined and springy curls that have a loopy, “S” shaped pattern

3c-4a

This hair has small curls or coils.

4b-4c

This hair texture is tightly coiled
